The Crooked Broom

Welcome to The Crooked Broom

Step into a world where every dish is crafted with enchantment and every sip holds a hint of mystery. Hidden from the eyes of the ordinary, The Crooked Broom is a gathering place for witches, wizards, and magical folk seeking a feast beyond the mundane.
Savor spellbinding dishes, sip handcrafted elixirs, and immerse yourself in an atmosphere where candlelight flickers with unseen whispers. Whether you’re here for a quiet potion by the fire or a grand banquet under an enchanted ceiling, magic is always on the menu.
Dine with us and discover flavors beyond this realm—what magic will you taste tonight?
