More than a thousand years of history

Founded in VII century in the heart of Eldermyst Woods by potion master Elowen Nightshade, The Crooked Broom soon became a legendary haven for witches and wizards seeking enchanted cuisine.
Over the centuries, it has welcomed renowned guests such as Merlin, Morgana Le Fay, and the elusive alchemist Nicolas Flamel. Event today, hidden from non-magical eyes, The Crooked Broom offers warmth and wonder to all who enter.
